Before you sign anything

Choosing an agency in Dubai

Check RERA registration

Every legitimate broker in Dubai must be registered with the Real Estate Regulatory Agency. Ask for the agent's BRN (Broker Registration Number) and verify it directly with Dubai Land Department before signing anything.

Verify a broker on DLD →

Match specialty to your goal

Off-plan, secondary sales, leasing and commercial are different disciplines. An agency with a strong track record in your target community will price and negotiate better than a generalist.

Understand the fees

Standard buyer agency commission in Dubai is typically 2% of purchase price. Tenants usually pay 5% of annual rent. Anything unusual should be explained in writing before you commit.

Frequently asked questions

Is this directory affiliated with any agency?

No. We're an independent directory — we don't work for or represent any of the agencies listed. Our footer says it plainly for a reason: independence is the whole point of comparing agencies in one place.

How are agencies selected for the free directory?

Standard listings are curated based on years established, market presence and specialty coverage across Dubai. Standard placement is never paid for and is never reordered by payment.

What exactly does "Featured" mean?

Featured Placement is a clearly labelled paid package: a gold badge, priority position at the top of results, and an enhanced profile. It never replaces or hides Standard listings — it's an addition, always disclosed with the ★ Featured badge.

Do you charge visitors anything?

No. Browsing, searching and filtering the directory is free and always will be — revenue comes only from agencies who opt into Featured Placement.

Where do the star ratings come from?

Each agency's rating and review count is its public Google Maps rating, checked by hand roughly once a week — not a live feed. Click any rating to see the current live number directly on Google. We show Google only; we don't blend in other platforms, since a blended "score" across different rating scales would be harder for you to verify yourself.

Can I suggest an agency to add?

Yes — email us at hello@dubairealestatedirectory.com with the agency name and we'll review it for the free directory.
