Choosing an agency in Dubai

Check RERA registration

Every legitimate broker in Dubai must be registered with the Real Estate Regulatory Agency. Ask for the agent's BRN (Broker Registration Number) and verify it on the Dubai REST app before signing anything.

Match specialty to your goal

Off-plan, secondary sales, leasing and commercial are different disciplines. An agency with a strong track record in your target community will price and negotiate better than a generalist.

Understand the fees

Standard buyer agency commission in Dubai is typically 2% of purchase price. Tenants usually pay 5% of annual rent. Anything unusual should be explained in writing before you commit.
