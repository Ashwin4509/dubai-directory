Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026
This is a standard privacy policy template. Before this site goes live and starts collecting real visitor data, it should be reviewed by a UAE-qualified lawyer to confirm compliance with the UAE's Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL, Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021) and any other applicable law.

Dubai Real Estate Directory ("we", "us", "our") operates dubairealestatedirectory.com (the "Site"). This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

What we collect

We collect information you choose to give us directly. The Site does not require an account or login for visitors.

  • Listing enquiries: if you submit the "Enquire About Listing" form, we receive the agency name, contact name, email address, phone number (optional), package selection, and any message you write. This is sent as an email to our inbox — we do not store it in a database.
  • Browsing data: standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) may be collected by our hosting provider for security and reliability purposes.

What we don't collect

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or advertising pixels at this time. If that changes, this policy will be updated first and, where required, we will ask for your consent.

Third-party services

This Site loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com, fonts.gstatic.com). Loading these fonts may share your IP address and browser information with Google as part of normal web font delivery. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

How we use your information

  • To respond to listing enquiries and manage your agency's directory listing.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the Site.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share listing-enquiry data with third parties except as needed to respond to your enquiry (e.g., our email provider).

Data retention

Enquiry emails are retained as long as reasonably needed to manage the business relationship with a listed agency, or until you ask us to delete them.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@dubairealestatedirectory.com.

Agency directory information

Information about listed real estate agencies (name, description, specialties, year established) is compiled from publicly available business information, not personal data about individuals. If you represent a listed agency and want information corrected or removed, contact us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

Contact
